# Plugins utilizados no software

> aws sdk
https://docs.aws.amazon.com/sdk-for-php/v3/developer-guide/getting-started_installation.html
composer require aws/aws-sdk-php --with-all-dependencies
-
> mpdf | pdf
https://github.com/kartik-v/yii2-mpdf
composer require kartik-v/yii2-mpdf "dev-master"
-
> select2
https://github.com/kartik-v/yii2-widget-select2
composer require kartik-v/yii2-widget-select2 "@dev"
-
> PhpOffice PhpSpreadsheet | excel
https://github.com/PHPOffice/PhpSpreadsheet
composer require phpoffice/phpspreadsheet
-
> kartik widgets
https://github.com/kartik-v/yii2-widgets
composer require kartik-v/yii2-widgets "*"
-
> Imagine | imagens
https://www.yiiframework.com/extension/yiisoft/yii2-imagine
composer require --prefer-dist yiisoft/yii2-imagine
